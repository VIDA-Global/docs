### How to Configure the Agent's Voice

To select the voice for your AI agent:
1. Navigate to the voice settings in your Vida agent configuration.
2. Browse the available voice options and listen to samples to choose the one that best fits your needs.
3. Select your preferred voice and save your settings to apply the changes.

### Tips for Choosing the Right Voice

- **Match Your Brand**: Choose a voice that reflects your brand’s personality and tone. For example, an energetic and engaging voice might be suitable for a casual eatery, while a calm and authoritative voice could be ideal for a professional service.
- **Consider Your Audience**: Think about what your customers might prefer. A clear and pleasant voice is generally easier to understand and can be more enjoyable for longer interactions.
- **Test Different Voices**: Don’t hesitate to try out several voices to see which one resonates best with your customers and suits various scenarios.

### Important Note

Certain AI TTS voices may not be available when Spanish is selected as the language. Ensure to review the available options under the chosen language settings.

By selecting the appropriate voice for your AI agent, you can enhance customer interactions and ensure that your communication is both effective and aligned with your brand's identity.
