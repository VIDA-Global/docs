### Cox Auto XTime

Cox Auto's XTime is the most used maintenance scheduling system by US auto dealerships. Enable this integration to allow your Vida AI agent to schedule and manage maintenance appointments for your customers over calls, sms, and emails. Email help@vida.inc if you are a dealership and would like to enable this integration.

### Cox Auto VinSolutions

Cox Auto's VinSolutions is the most used CRM by US auto dealerships. Enable this integration to allow your Vida AI agent to interact with Leads and Contacts in your CRM when handling calls, sms, and emails. Great for lead qualification. Email help@vida.inc if you are a dealership and would like to enable this integration.
